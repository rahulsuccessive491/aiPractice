What's Built

Everything a manager needs
to see AI adoption in real time

Leadership Dashboard

Executive overview of total users, weekly/monthly activity trends, team adoption rates, and certification progress — all in one screen.

Admin only Highcharts

Team Breakdown

Filter by team, drill into individual developers. Compare adoption percentages, see who is active, and spot laggards at a glance.

Manager view 12 teams

Activity Logging

Developers self-report AI tool usage, learning sessions, and experiments. Categorised by domain, tool, and type with duplicate prevention.

Dev facing Tagged

Certification Tracker

Track AI certifications org-wide. Developers mark certs as in-progress or completed; admins see the full picture across all teams.

All roles Status tracking

Role-Based Access

Three-tier RBAC: Developers log their own work, Managers view their teams, Admins see everything. Gated to @successive.tech emails.

Secure JWT auth

CSV Export

One-click export of all activity data for offline analysis. Managers can pull team reports; admins get the full org-level dump.

Reporting Excel ready
